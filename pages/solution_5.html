Natural Language Processing
Anmerkung enables NLP researchers to quickly extract value from text by providing a platform agnostic, trained workforce partner for unbiased, high quality annotations on vast amounts of text-based data.

It’s an essential first step to further NLP tasks, such as document classification or sentiment analysis, by teaching NLP models what to look for in text.
